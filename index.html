Our Services

We have served our customers for over 10 years.

Korean food

Korean food is some of the healthiest on earth, with an emphasis on vegetables, meats cooked simply and without much oil, and a near obsession with the kimchi.

Japanese food

The freshness is one of the major factors that makes a difference in how sushi tastes. We go everyday to the local fish market like Tsukiji Fish Market to pick up the days seafood.

American food

American foods are fairly bland and typically full of fat and sugar which makes them taste good. We have the best Western chefs who can serve pizza, burgers, and salads.

Catering Service

Our full-service kitchen and dedicated team will help plan your event. Our catering has an expanded menu with Japanese sushi, Korean cuisine, American finger foods.
